## Pricing Psychology

### Why $15/month Works:
1. **Affordable barrier to entry** - Anyone can try it
2. **$0.50/day** - "Less than a coffee"
3. **Commitment sweet spot** - Enough to take seriously, not intimidating
4. **Room to upgrade** - Credit packs available for power users
5. **Simple number** - Easy to remember and share

### Why NOT $10 or $20:
- **$10** = Too cheap, not sustainable at scale
- **$20** = Psychological barrier for casual users
- **$15** = Perfect middle ground
