## üìö The Learning Journey

### 1. **11x LOVE Code Masterclass** (Foundation)
- **What:** The foundational course teaching all 11 Dimensions
- **When:** Complete once (or annual refresh)
- **Structure:** INTRO + 5 Modules + BONUS
  - **INTRO: BELIEVE** ‚Äî Welcome to the 1st Day of Your New Life
  - **Module 1: BREAKTHROUGH** ‚Äî Discover & Define (Identity, MEhD, Ikigai)
  - **Module 2: BREAKDOWN** ‚Äî Detective: WTF Just Happened (Reconnect to LOVE, Life Audit, 1st Obituary)
  - **Module 3: BRAVERY** ‚Äî Decide & Delete (Soul Contract, Cosmic Council, Freedom Funeral)
  - **Module 4: BUILD** ‚Äî Design & Destiny (Vision Board on Steroids, Treasure Map, 2nd Obituary)
  - **Module 5: BADASSERY** ‚Äî Do or Do Not (5 V's Daily Practice, Buddy Up, 4 C's)
  - **BONUS: TREASURE CHEST** ‚Äî Power-Tools for Daily Practice

### 2. **Daily LOVE Practice** (The Engine)
- **Morning:** The 5 V's (~11 minutes)
  1. **Vibe** ‚Äî What's my vibe today? Song of the day?
  2. **Vision** ‚Äî What am I creating? Focus for today?
  3. **Value** ‚Äî What matters most? How will I contribute?
  4. **Villain** ‚Äî What could stop me? Counter-tool?
  5. **Victory** ‚Äî (evening) What's my win today?
  
- **Evening:** LaB Reflection (~5 minutes)
  - CELEBRATIONS! ‚Äî What wins did I have?
  - LESSONS ‚Äî What did I learn?
  - BLESSINGS ‚Äî What am I grateful for?
  - DREAM VIBES ‚Äî Set vibe for sleep/tomorrow

### 3. **Weekly Experiments** (The Playground)
- **What:** Weekly themed exploration within a Dimension
- **Format:** Hypothesis ‚Üí Method (daily Discoveries) ‚Üí Results ‚Üí Conclusion
- **Energy:** "I wonder what happens if..." ‚Äî curiosity over criticism
- **Example:** "The Morning Miracle ‚Äî Testing What Actually Works Before 8am"

### 4. **EQ Visualizer** (Progress Dashboard)
- **Visual:** Graphic equalizer with 11 bars (one per Dimension)
- **Shows:** Current level vs. Big Dream target in each Dimension
- **Why a DJ metaphor?** "You're the DJ of your life ‚Äî raise your vibes!"

### Custom Nostr Event Kinds

Use `kind: 30078` (replaceable app-specific data) for all user progress events.

#### Experiment Completion
```javascript
{
  kind: 30078,
  tags: [
    ["d", "experiment-complete"],
    ["experiment", "11x-love-code"],
    ["lesson", "module-1-lesson-3"],
    ["completed_at", "2026-02-11T18:00:00Z"]
  ],
  content: nip44.encrypt(JSON.stringify({
    experimentId: "11x-love-code",
    lessonId: "module-1-lesson-3",
    completedAt: "2026-02-11T18:00:00Z"
  }), userKey)
}
```

#### Daily Check-in (5 V's)
```javascript
{
  kind: 30078,
  tags: [
    ["d", "daily-checkin"],
    ["date", "2026-02-11"],
    ["dimension", "body"]
  ],
  content: nip44.encrypt(JSON.stringify({
    date: "2026-02-11",

    value: "health",

```
